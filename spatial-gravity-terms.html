Privacy Policy for Spatial Gravity

Last updated: October 19, 2025

Overview

Spatial Gravity is an augmented reality (AR) experience designed for Android XR headsets. The app allows users to view virtual planets in their physical space using camera input.

Data Collection and Use

Spatial Gravity does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The app only uses the device camera and spatial sensors to enable AR functionality — specifically to display virtual planets in real-world environments.

Camera Access

The camera is used exclusively for rendering virtual objects. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted or stored on external servers.

Third-Party Services

Spatial Gravity does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data exposure. All processing occurs locally on the user’s XR device.

Children’s Privacy

Spatial Gravity is not specifically directed toward children, but it can be used by all audiences. No personal data from children or any users is collected.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

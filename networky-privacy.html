Networky — Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 5, 2025

Summary

Networky is designed to teach and visualize networking concepts without collecting or storing personal information. Networky does not collect, transmit, or retain any user data.

What this means

  • No personal data collection: We do not ask for or store names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No usage tracking: The app does not include analytics, telemetry, or any background tracking services.
  • No cloud sync or servers: Networky operates locally on your device and does not upload activity, projects, or settings to any server.
  • No third-party trackers: There are no advertising SDKs, analytics providers, or third-party trackers embedded in Networky.
